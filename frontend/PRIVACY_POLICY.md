# Privacy Policy for CBE Plan

**Last Updated:** December 2025

**Developer:** Legit Labs  
**Contact:** legitlabs@outlook.com

---

## Introduction

CBE Plan ("we," "our," or "us") is a Competency-Based Education lesson planning application designed for Kenyan teachers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- First name and last name
- School name
- Password (encrypted)

### 2. Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Lesson plans you generate
- Teaching notes and schemes of work created
- App usage patterns and preferences

### 3. Payment Information
For wallet top-ups via M-Pesa:
- Phone number (for M-Pesa STK Push)
- Transaction references
- Payment amounts and timestamps

**Note:** We do not store your M-Pesa PIN or full payment credentials. All payments are processed securely through Safaricom's Daraja API.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Generate personalized lesson plans aligned with KICD curriculum
- Process wallet top-ups and track your balance
- Provide customer support
- Improve our services and user experience
- Send important service updates

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage
- Your data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas cloud servers
- Authentication is handled through Firebase Authentication
- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS

### Security Measures
- Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
- API endpoints are protected with JWT authentication
- Regular security updates and monitoring

---

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- **Safaricom (M-Pesa):** For payment processing only
- **Firebase (Google):** For authentication services only
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or legal process

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct your information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability:** Export your lesson plans and data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **legitlabs@outlook.com**

---

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Lesson plans and teaching materials are retained for your reference
- You may request deletion of your data at any time
- Upon account deletion, your data will be removed within 30 days

---

## Children's Privacy

CBE Plan is intended for use by teachers and educators. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.
